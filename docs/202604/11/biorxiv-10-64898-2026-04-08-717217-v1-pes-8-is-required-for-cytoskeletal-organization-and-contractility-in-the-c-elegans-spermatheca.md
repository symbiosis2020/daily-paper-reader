## 摘要
生物管道的收缩与舒张调节对机体功能至关重要。在秀丽隐杆线虫中，由平滑肌样细胞组成的受精囊会随着卵母细胞的通过而反复拉伸与收缩。本文报道了PES-8——一种先前未表征的蛋白质——作为受精囊收缩功能的调控因子。PES-8含有一个预测的细胞外透明带样结构域和一个非结构化的胞质尾区，提示其在细胞外和胞质信号传导中具有双重作用。PES-8定位于受精囊、受精囊-子宫瓣膜及子宫的质膜上。功能分析表明，PES-8对受精囊功能至关重要；其缺失会破坏肌动球蛋白纤维排列、FLN-1/细丝蛋白定位、顶端连接组织以及钙信号传导，从而阻碍卵母细胞的通过。这些发现确立了PES-8是秀丽隐杆线虫受精囊中细胞骨架组织和钙介导收缩功能的关键调控因子。

## Abstract
Proper regulation of contraction and relaxation in biological tubes is essential for organismal function. In C. elegans, the spermatheca, composed of smooth muscle-like cells, undergoes repeated stretching and contraction as oocytes pass through. Here we describe PES-8, a previously uncharacterized protein, as a regulator of spermatheca contractility. PES-8 contains a predicted extracellular zona pellucida-like domain and an unstructured cytoplasmic tail, suggesting dual roles in extracellular and cytoplasmic signaling. PES-8 localizes to the plasma membrane of the spermatheca, the spermathecal-uterine valve, and uterus. Functional analysis shows that PES-8 is essential for spermathecal function; its loss disrupts actomyosin fiber alignment, FLN-1/filamin localization, apical junction organization, and calcium signaling, preventing oocyte transit. These findings identify PES-8 as a key regulator of cytoskeletal organization and calcium-mediated contractility in the C. elegans spermatheca.
